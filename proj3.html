&Pav

A branding project for a new restaurant
An idea



Indian people love things that are multi faceted. We love things that fit just right into our life and become a part of it easily without any struggle or adjustment perse. &Pav is that combination. It's is food, that is delicious and healthy combined slightly new version of our Desi dishes. &Pav brings to you this collection of all Pav related dishes where Pav is the spotlight. Just like the warmth of Pav, freshly out of the oven, &Pav respects its Mumbai roots and the ‘bhidu wali Mumbai dosti’ which is the main focus for promoting this brand.

‘Pav’ meaning bread in hindi is a beloved dish of Mumbai. &Pav is an intiative where the people of Mumbai can taste the goodness of Bombay in every bite. It is definetely a place of chill for hanging out, yet it also serves delicious and quick ready to go meals. Pav is definetely the hero here, Thus, all other ingredients respect it so much that even throughout the menu they change but one thing stays constant - ‘Pav’

I am not the only one crazy about food in the family. It is practically everyone who thinks like me; A very good example is baba (my father). He is the craziest when it comes to food combinations and his wildest thoughts about such combinations is perhaps where &Pav took birth.&Pav is one of my father’s ideas for a startup, crazy right! I was always keen and attached to it whenever we spoke and finally I decided that it was about to time for this dream/passion project to have it’s own visual identity!

And yes the final outcome was nothing short from crazy. It was my first time designing a visual identity and I had a ball. It was amazing!
